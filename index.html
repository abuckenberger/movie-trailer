IT HAPPENED ONE NIGHT

Frank Capra's 1934 film It Happened One Night, staring Clark Gable and Claudette Colbert brought screwball comedy to the film industry. The two strikingly different stars cross paths and spend the entirety of the film on a journey together. Despite their differences...their travels, wit, and sense of security bring them together, ultimately ending in romance.
